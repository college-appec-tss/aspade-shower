About ASPADE

Empowering students with education, technology, and discipline.

ASPADE Ngororero Secondary School offers excellent learning opportunities for students in O'Level and Advanced Level. The school encourages leadership, academic excellence, creativity, and practical computer skills. Through modern teaching methods and a supportive environment, students are prepared for university, technology careers, and future innovation.

School Levels

Quality education pathways designed for student success.

O'Level

Strong academic foundation in Mathematics, Sciences, ICT, Languages, and Humanities to prepare students for Advanced Level education.

Advanced Level

Specialized combinations such as MCE and MEG help students develop skills in technology, economics, programming, geography, and research.
